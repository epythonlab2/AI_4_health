AI for Healthcare

Bridging clinical knowledge and machine learning practice to design responsible health solutions.

Level

Intermediate

Foundational to Applied

Total Time

108 Hours

12 Weeks • 3 Sessions/wk

Delivery

Hybrid

On-campus & Online

Learning Objectives

Key Outcomes

  • Explain the role of machine learning in modern healthcare systems
  • Prepare and analyze healthcare datasets for machine learning tasks
  • Build supervised and unsupervised learning models for clinical use cases
  • Evaluate models using clinically meaningful metrics
  • Apply predictive modeling for disease prevention and health system planning
  • Address ethical, fairness, and deployment challenges in healthcare AI
  • Communicate findings effectively to clinical and technical stakeholders

Accreditation

  • Outcomes-based learning objectives
  • Emphasis on ethical and responsible AI
  • Practical workforce-relevant skills
  • Suitable for academic credit, CPD/CME, and funded capacity-building programs.
